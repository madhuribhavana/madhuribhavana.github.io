UX @ CARDEKHO.COM

Improving the car buying experience

As a UXer, I was responsible for understanding car buyers' needs and behaviors and design meaningful solutions to enhance their car buying experience.

THE GOALS..

• To understand user types and experience
• To improve usability across all product platforms
• To personalize user journeys to deliver a meaningful experience

MY WORK

What did I do?

MIXED METHODS RESEARCH

Conducted research to understand user types, their behaviours, needs, painpoints and touchpoints.

• SME interviews to understand product vision and goals. • Contextual inquiries with users • Interviews with stakeholders • Card sorting • UI Evaluation via heuristics

QUANT RESEARCH

Quant research to track user conversions and analyze user journeys.

• Used Google analytics to track journey related metrics • Used NPS ratings to understand painpoints in experiences • In-page surveys

ANALYSIS AND USER TYPES

Created personas for the product and generated behavioural archetypes

• Broke down the current journeys into broad stages - research, dealer interaction and purchase and represented the touchpoints and painpoints.

PRODUCT DESIGN

Designed new features, changed information architecture and personalized user experiences.

Colloborated with product, FE, content and visual design teams to design and deploy new features.

EVALUATION AND TESTING

Tracked and tested design changes via qual and quantitative methods.

Based on the testing results, iterations to the new features were made.

VISUAL DESIGN

Designed and developed a visual style guide and component library.

Colloborated with the FE team to develop a component library for cardekho's subsidiary - Zigwheels.com.
